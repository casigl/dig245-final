Hi there! Dream-Bot is a project interested in uncovering the meanings buried in our dreams. Take the results of the bot with a grain of salt- I am very curious about dream psychology but understand that a lot of it can feel like pseudoscience!

While there are resources online to understand certain symbols found in dreams, it can be hard to find a comprehensive analysis. I was motivated to create a personable & playful interface that not only gives insight to each symbol all in one place but also reacts to your dream.
